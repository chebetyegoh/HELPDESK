FAQS

How do I sign in?

- For signing in you must register an account first if you do not have an account then sign in with your username and password.

How do I raise a ticket?

- First sign in into your account, then on the left there is a menu.
- Click on the Raise Ticket.
- Enter your registration number, ticket name, ticket priority and a ticket description then press the raise ticket button.

How do I track a ticket?

- Once you have raised a ticket our officer will be notified via email and once your issue is solved, that is, the ticket is closed you will recieve an emiail

How do I delete a ticket?

- After logging in on the menu go to My Tickets then click on view ticket button.
- This will lead you to a page with your ticket details.
- At the bottom of the page there is a delete ticket click on it.
- This pops up a dialogue that asks you if you are sure you want to delete the ticket.
- If yes click on 'Yes I'm sure' button this will delete the ticket.

How do I edit a ticket?

- After logging in on the menu go to My Tickets then click on view ticket button.
- This will lead you to a page with your ticket details.
- At the botom of the page click on update ticket then fill in the fields then click update.
- Your ticket will be updated sucessfully.
